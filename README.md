## Featured Projects

### 1. SME Financing & Growth Analysis (Time Series Econometrics)
Analyzed national-level SME financing trends (2016–2024) using SARIMA and SARIMAX models.  
Identified that structured credit (trade finance & public lending) significantly drives SME growth, while borrower volume alone has limited impact.  
**Impact:** Delivered policy-relevant insights for financial decision-making.

---

### 2. Credit Card Spend Modeling & Revenue Driver Analysis
Built and evaluated multiple regression models on 5,000+ customers to identify key revenue drivers.  
Compared model performance using R², Adjusted R², and RMSE to select the most robust model.  
**Impact:** Enabled data-driven revenue optimization and customer targeting.

---

### 3. Impact of Beer Tax on Traffic Fatalities (Panel Data Analysis)
Conducted econometric analysis using Fixed Effects and Random Effects models in R.  
Examined how beer taxation influences traffic fatalities across U.S. states.  
**Impact:** Demonstrated application of policy analysis and causal inference using real-world panel data.

---

### 4. Haunted Places Dashboard (Data Visualization)
Developed an interactive dashboard mapping haunted locations across the U.S.  
Analyzed spatial patterns and state-wise distribution of reported encounters.  
**Impact:** Showcased storytelling through data visualization and geographic analysis.

---

### 5. Titanic Survival Prediction (Machine Learning Classification)
Built and compared Logistic Regression, LDA, QDA, Naive Bayes, and kNN models.  
Achieved ~79% accuracy and ~0.88 AUC with strong interpretability of key survival factors.  
**Impact:** Demonstrated model comparison, evaluation, and feature importance analysis.

---

### 6. Predicting Life Expectancy (Tree-Based Models)
Developed Regression Trees, Bagging, and Random Forest models on global data (2014–2015).  
Evaluated performance using cross-validation and MSE metrics.  
**Impact:** Identified key socio-economic drivers such as adult mortality and GDP.
